About Our Company

POSSIBILITY MARITIME is an indigenous Nigeria company, trusted registered with the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) with RC: 1941474. A leading Shipping Company providing high quality marine Services in Nigeria, present in all the major sea ports in Nigeria. Our core business is to provide quality marine service operations in Nigeria.

PMSL is emerging as a leading regional marine solutions provider capable of blending international standards with local knowledge. PMSL strives to provide quality and proficient services to the clients, realizing the needs to have professional services at most effective manner.

We have vast experience in the Marine industry with a team of Master Mariners, Marine Engineers, Marine Surveyors, Logistics Managers, Operational Staff and Supply Chain Managers to take care of all the marine needs of our esteemed clients.

vision

to be the foremost shipping company in Africa that will be most admitted for its excellent, efficient and professional services.

to provide a world class range of professional quality services in the maritime industry to our would be clients.
