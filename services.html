OUR SHIPPING AGENCY SERVICES

  • SHIP AGENCY SERVICES: we represent principals in oil and gas industry in safe berthing and sailing of their chartered tanker.
  • BUNKER SUPPLY: we are engaged in supply of bunkers and fresh water to vessels that call to Nigerian ports with minimum competitive price
  • PROTECTIVE AGENCY: we act on behalf of ship owners to ensure hitch free vessels operations in all Nigerian ports. To carry out proper repairs of equipment and outfits of ship for a smooth voyage, easing the stress of ship owners in certification of ships and revalidation of necessary ship documents.
  • SHIP BROKERAGE & CHARTERING: we are positioned in connecting ship owners prospective charters.
  • SHIP TO SHIP (STS) OPERATION: provide logistics support and marine equipment’s for a safe ship to ship cargo transfer.
  • MARINE EQUIPMENT LEASING: – Involved in marine equipment leasing such as fenders, tugboats, hoses, service boats among others.
  • PRODUCT SOURCING: – Involved in assisting protective clients in sourcing for petroleum products as would be needed from time to time
  • SHIP HANDLING: Involved in supply of victuals and all kind of food stuff to vessels at best competitive price
  • CASH TO MASTER: Assist ship owners in supply of cash to masters as at when required.
  • SLOP REMOVAL: Involved in removal of ship slop from vessels when need be
  • FREIGHT AND CLEARING: we have a team of experienced freight forwarders to handle any size or sharp or weight of cargo from anywhere in the world.
  • ESCORT SERVICES Liaise with approved government escort service agency for escort services
  • CREW CHANGE: Provide up to date acquaintance with government regulations via Nigeria immigration service on crew matters, execute crew change service for clients.
